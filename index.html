INTEGRATIVE LEADERSHIP IN HIGHER EDUCATION

strategic, ethical, corporate responsible and inclusive

Objectives

Goals and objectives

Strengthen the leadership of higher university managers in the Dominican Republic an active, dynamic and strategic role, allowing their development in the current position and growth to occupy positions of greater responsibility; also, that it contributes to the long-term development of its institutions and the implementation of an ethical, inclusive and socially responsible management.

Objectives

Induce the participant to self-knowledge, by self-assessment of their leadership skills , and promote improvement actions for their personal and professional development based on the results of their evaluation.

Strengthen the participants' soft skills , specifically the leadership skills: strategic thinking, communication, emotional intelligence, great commitment, among all.

Integrate in the participants the ethical and inclusive sense and the social responsibility so that they can apply it both in the management of their units, as well as in the behavior and actions of their team.
